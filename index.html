About Us

We have been in business since 2022. Our Salmon Cookies combines two of our founder's passions; his love for savory confections, and his commitment towards conservation of healthy marine ecosystems. Our cookies are suitable for both humans and salmons to enjoy, and come in an assortment of mouth-watering sweet and/or savory flavors.

Our original confections go perfectly with a cold glass of milk, an early morning coffee, or a hot cappuccino. Flavors include Brown Sugar & Honey Salmon, Lemon Zesty Salmon, and Mild Sweet Salmon.

Our newly launched savory line is perfect for a mid-afternoon snack and pairs perfectly with a light and crisp green or jasmine tea, or a cold brew. Our savory flavors include Original Sea Salt Salmon, Blackened Salmon, Smoked Salmon, Spicy Salmon & Seaweed Salmon, Three-Flavor Salmon & Basil, and Ultimate Salmon Heaven.

There is a perfect flavor for every member of your family to enjoy!
